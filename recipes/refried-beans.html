Refried Beans

Description

This simple, fat free recipe will yield some of the most delicious refried beans you've ever tasted.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry pinto beans
  • 6 cups water
  • ½ fresh jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 small onion, peeled and cut in half

Steps

  1. Rinse beans in a fine mesh strainer. Pour beans into the cooking pot of a slow cooker.
  2. Add water, minced jalapeño, garlic, salt, and pepper to slow cooker. Stir to combine.
  3. Place onion halves open side down on top of the beans.
  4. Cover and cook on high until beans are well cooked and completely soft, at least 8 hours.
  5. Cover and cook on high until beans are well cooked and completely soft, at least 8 hours.
  6. Remove onion chunks and mash beans with a potato masher.
  7. Serve with rice, chips, tortillas or as an ingredient in your favorite Mexican dish.
