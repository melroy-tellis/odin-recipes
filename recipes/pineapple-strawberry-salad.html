Pineapple Strawberry Salad

Description

The tropical flavor of pineapple combined with the sweet freshness of strawberries is the perfect greeting to summer. This simple salad has several nuanced layers of flavor, making it great either as a salsa or a side salad. If making it for a salsa, finely dice all ingredients. If making for a side salad, dice and chop into slightly larger chunks.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, diced
  • 2 cups fresh pineapple, chopped
  • ½ small red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ medium sweet red pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro or parsley
  • 2 tablespoons key lime juice
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt, or to taste
  • dash of pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Optionally refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight for best flavors.
